PunchClockPanda patch 2

Fixes:
- Added database migration to remove the old 4 dummy employees and default admin PIN 9999 when upgrading from the seeded build.
- Disabled Android backup/restore for app data so uninstall/reinstall and fresh installs behave like true local-only installs.
- Added data extraction rules to keep databases and files out of cloud/device restore.

Why this was needed:
- The prior patch removed seeding for brand-new databases, but existing installs could still keep or restore old seeded data.
